#Format
Presentation of 10-12 minutes followed by feedback
Language: English
High-level presentation — avoid technicalities  as much as possible; speak to stakeholders, not engineers
bring your storytelling - explain your project to stockholders, not to engineers. keep it simple and short.
Slides only (no notebooks or Word files or raw documents)
This is version 0 of your final presentation

#Topics to include
Intro slide: project name, title, team member names, etc.
Business goal and motivation - the problem, the target users, and why it matters
Success criteria/KPIs - how will you measure whether the product works?
Project roadmap with milestones - what's done, what's in progress, what's next
Evaluation metric
Data collection source and challenges — description of columns, shape, etc.
EDA with plots
ML workflow / data pipeline
Baseline model and its performance
Thank you slide
Optional: empty slides referencing next week's work — advanced model, future work, etc.
